    • I am an Assistant Professor in the Network Intelligence Center (NICE), School of Information Science and Technology (SIST) at the ShanghaiTech University. Previously, I was a post-doctoral research fellow in The University of Hong Kong and Communications and Signal Processing Group at Imperial College London, under the guidance of Prof. Bruno Clerckx (IEEE Fellow). I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from The University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Victor O.K. Li (IEEE Life Fellow).

    Research Interests

    • Artifical Intelligence-empowered Wireless Networks
